What is CJP Wiki?

CJP Wiki is a public transparency and relationship intelligence platform that helps people explore publicly available information, timelines, relationships, organizational structures, political history, investigations, tenders, and evidence-backed records in one place.

📄

Evidence Based

Every visible claim or relationship is backed by publicly available sources or evidence.

🕸️

Relationship Mapping

Trace direct and indirect relationships between people, companies, and organizations.

📅

Timeline Tracking

Explore important events, appointments, investigations, affiliations, and changes over time.

🏛️

Public Profiles

View profiles of political candidates, organizations, companies, public entities, and more.

🔍

Source Transparency

Inspect attached sources, evidence links, public records, and historical revisions.

🌍

Community Driven

Built as an open and collaborative public knowledge platform for transparency and accountability.
